About Us

Saini Shutterings and Scaffolding's.

Saini Shuttering Pvt Ltd founded by Mr. Mukesh Saini in 2000 is a well established company dealing in Scaffolding and Shuttering materials on hire and contract basis. We supply the shuttering and scaffolding material for the construction of projects like house, shops, housing societies, factories, commercial complexes,townships, throughout the country at very competitive rates. Firm is in existence since last 20 years and has reached today at a pioneer level through the efforts & hard work of its Proprietor Mr. Mukesh Saini and his experienced team. It has established itself as one of the most prominent and reliable player in the Shutterings and Scaffolding business in North India specially in Farrukhnagar, Pataudi, Jhajjar and Gurgaon .
